Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to recommend white water rafting locations in Idaho and California. OR to teach beginners what they need to know and what they need to have to make their first white water rafting adventure.

Audience

v1: Our audience will be people who are new to white water rafting and want to explore options in the Idaho and California areas and who are college-aged. v2: Our target customers are college students looking for outdoor adventure within/around the Rexburg area at affordable prices. Typical site users will be accessing from a laptop environment.
